## Iaido

### Documents

- [Idaho Kendo Club > Iaido Resources](https://www.idaho-kendo.org/iaido) | Includes side-by-side translations of various documents including the ZNKR Iai Instruction Manual and ZNKR Iaido Shinpan Rules.

- [2025 ZNKR Iai Points to Note When Teaching](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1lz92a4-jzagoyrAiyXebYbkc5F45anX0SzLgE0YAOb4) | All Japan Kendo Federation Iai "Points to Note When Teaching" translation by Andy Watson and John Burn.

### Video's

- [Iaido in Amersfoort](https://www.youtube.com/@iaidoinamersfoort) | This channel covers a range of subjects such as how to tie your hakama, where the opponents are in kata, and even how to set up competition fields.
- [All Japan Kendo Federation](https://www.youtube.com/@ZennipponKendoRenmei/playlists) | Recordings of all ZNKR organised competitions.
