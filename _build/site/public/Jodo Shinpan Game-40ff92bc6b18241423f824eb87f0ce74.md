# Jodo Shinpan Game

Shinpan Shobu consists of a collection of matches and the video will pause just before the flags are shown, giving you the chance to give your desision before being influenced by the official results.

The matches are selected from the all japan jodo championships 2024 and 2025, all matches have a unanimous winner. The goal is to help sharpen your eyes by refereeing along with experienced shinpan.
