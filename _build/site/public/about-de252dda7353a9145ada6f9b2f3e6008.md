# A collection of tools to learn at home.

We are expected to pick up quite a bit of information during practice, such as commands, kata names and terms. Being able to quickly recall these helps free up mental space in the dojo for actual pracrtice. The goal of this website is to help you study at home, so you can get the most out of your time in the dojo.
